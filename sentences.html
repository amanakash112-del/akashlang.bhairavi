Joylang Idioms & Cultural Expressions

A language is not complete without idiomatic expressions. These are Joylang's core idioms — they use metaphor, sound symbolism, and cultural warmth.

Sato-ko mavi dilo.
Literal: Give water to friend.
English equivalent: Break the ice.
Rona-chi velo.
Literal: Go with love.
English equivalent: Wear your heart on your sleeve.
Kachi-pachu melo.
Literal: Be five-eating.
English equivalent: Bite off more than you can chew.
Jovi-ombu bolonu melo.
Literal: Be a big joy-thing.
English equivalent: Be the life of the party.
Mavi-me nashilo.
Literal: Learn in water.
English equivalent: Learn by doing / sink or swim.
Anu nalu-me yochilo.
Literal: I think in sleep.
English equivalent: Let me sleep on it.
Shoju-chi shapilo.
Literal: Speak with laughter.
English equivalent: Laugh your way through it.
Sato nishi melo.
Literal: Be near a friend.
English equivalent: Have someone's back.
Kachi boshi velo.
Literal: Go away from eating.
English equivalent: Burn bridges.
Goloku rona melo.
Literal: Good love exists.
English equivalent: All is well that ends well.

Sample Conversations

Dialogue 1 — Meeting a Friend

A:
Namashte! Ka tenu jovioku melo?
Hello! Are you happy?
B:
Anu jovioku melo! Krupanu. Ka tenu?
I am happy! Thank you. And you?
A:
Anu ajunu boloku melo — anu satoombu-ko doshivilo.
I am very well today — I have just seen a friend.
B:
Sachi melu? Ka oshi kenu?
Really? Who was that?
A:
Oshi Lena — anuchi vidyombu-me nashishime.
That was Lena — we were studying at school.

Dialogue 2 — Travel & Plans

A:
Ka tenu nagombu-ta belo?
Did you come from the city?
B:
Anu ve, anuchi gramombu-ta belo.
No, we came from the village.
A:
Ka tenu vidyombu-me nashishilo?
Were you studying at school?
B:
Anu, anu satoombu komi nashishilo.
Yes, I was studying with a friend.
A:
Goloku! Anuchi kalanu komi nashilobu?
Great! Shall we study together tomorrow?
B:
Anu jovioku! Kalanu doshibo.
I would be happy! I'll see you tomorrow.

Dialogue 3 — Learning Joylang

A:
Ka tenu Joylang nashishilo?
Are you learning Joylang?
B:
Anu! Anu nashilo teva boloku shitioku melo.
Yes! I am learning but it is somewhat hard.
A:
Vaho! Anu sahibo. Henu nashishilo?
Wow! I will help. How are you studying?
B:
Anu pustombu chi webombu yomilo.
I use books and the internet.
A:
Goloku! Nashi sadanu nashilo, tashi samilo.
Good! If you always study, then you will understand.
